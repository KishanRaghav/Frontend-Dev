Importance of Good Typography

Typography plays a major role in how users consume information on the web. A well-designed text layout improves readability, visual appeal, and user experience. Styling text using spacing, alignment and font variations enhances clarity. Additionally, highlighted text can help draw attention to key points.

Good typography ensures that content is easy to read and visually balanced. Designers often experiment with font-weights, font-styles, and different font families to create hierarchy and structure.
